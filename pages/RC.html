Original Ratchet and Clank


Made by Insomniac Games and published by Sony Computer Entertainment for the PS2, in 2002. Ratchet and Clank is one of the most iconic of games that spawned an entire franchise .Recently it even had a 3D animated film released, featuring the voices of high profile celebrities like Paul Giamatti and Rosario Dawson.
